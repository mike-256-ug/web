Portfolio / Projects

Lt Col BT Okol has led and participated in numerous ICT, cyber security, and defence modernization initiatives within the Uganda People’s Defence Forces (UPDF). His projects reflect a commitment to secure communication systems, digital transformation, and capacity building for future military readiness.
