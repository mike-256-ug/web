Portfolio / Projects

Mr. Tom Benard Okol has led and contributed to several ICT initiatives at the National Council of Sports (NCS). His projects focus on digital transformation, sports information management systems, and capacity building to support the modernization of sports administration in Uganda.

Selected Projects & Initiatives

Key Projects (2021 - 2024)
Project Description Year
Sports Data Management System Developed a centralized digital platform for athlete registration, federation records, and performance tracking at NCS. 2024
ICT Infrastructure Upgrade Implemented LAN/WAN architecture, secure firewalls, and cloud backup systems to support NCS operations and federations nationwide. 2023
Stadium Wi-Fi & Networking Designed and deployed high-speed internet connectivity and digital ticketing systems for national sports facilities. 2022
ICT Capacity Building Program Developed training modules and mentorship programs to build ICT skills among staff and sports federation officials. 2021
