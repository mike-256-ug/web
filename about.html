Who is BT Okol...

Personal and Academic Background

Lt Col BT Okol is a senior officer in the Uganda People’s Defence Forces (UPDF) with a distinguished career in military communications, cyber security, and IT management.

He graduated from the Senior Command and Staff College, psc (U) and has completed numerous specialized courses in cyber defence, information security governance, and ICT systems integration both locally and internationally.

Professional Experience

He has served as Head of Communications and Information Security, Director of Communication and Information Technology (DCITS) – Land Forces, and Commanding Officer of various CITS Regiments. His work has focused on strengthening secure communication networks, safeguarding sensitive military data, and enhancing cyber resilience.

Leadership & Contributions

Beyond operational roles, Lt Col Okol has contributed to ICT policy formulation, infrastructure development, and mentoring young officers. His leadership has been key in modernizing UPDF’s C4ISR systems, improving operational efficiency and national security readiness.
