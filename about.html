Who is Mr. Tom Benard Okol?



Mr. Tom Benard Okol
IT Officer – National Council of Sports
Expert in Sports ICT Systems & Cyber Security

Mr. Tom Benard Okol is a dedicated Information Technology Officer with the National Council of Sports (NCS), where he plays a key role in driving digital transformation within Uganda’s sports sector. With a strong background in sports ICT systems, cyber security, and ICT infrastructure management, he is recognized for combining technical expertise with innovative solutions to strengthen sports administration and athlete management.

Personal and Academic Background

Mr. Okol is a graduate of advanced ICT training programs in information security, database management, and systems integration. He has also completed professional courses in sports information technology and digital governance, both in Uganda and internationally. This diverse academic and professional background equips him with the ability to design and implement innovative ICT solutions that meet the dynamic needs of the sports industry.

Professional Career

At the National Council of Sports, he has successfully led initiatives such as:

  • Development of athlete registration and data management systems
  • Deployment of ICT infrastructure in stadiums and sports federations
  • Implementation of cybersecurity frameworks to safeguard sensitive sports information

Through these projects, he has strengthened NCS’s digital capacity and improved efficiency in service delivery to sports federations and athletes.

Leadership & Contributions

Beyond technical work, Mr. Okol has been instrumental in:

  • Shaping ICT and digital policies within the sports sector
  • Guiding ICT infrastructure modernization projects
  • Mentoring and training young ICT professionals and sports administrators

His leadership continues to promote transparency, innovation, and professionalism in Uganda’s sports ICT ecosystem.

Achievements

Some of his notable achievements include:

  1. Establishing a digital database for athletes and federations
  2. Introducing secure online registration and ticketing platforms
  3. Enhancing ICT support for major national and regional sports events

Vision & Values

Mr. Okol is committed to advancing digital transformation in sports, ensuring data security, and building capacity among ICT professionals. He values integrity, teamwork, and innovation, and envisions a sports sector where technology drives both performance and transparency.
