Who is Lt Col BT Okol?



Lt Col BT Okol
Senior UPDF Officer
Expert in Cyber Security & Military Communications

Lt Col BT Okol is a seasoned senior officer in the Uganda People’s Defence Forces (UPDF) with over two decades of professional experience in military communication systems, cyber security, and information technology management. He is widely recognized for his ability to combine strategic leadership with advanced technical expertise to support national defence and security modernization.

Personal and Academic Background

Lt Col Okol is a graduate of the prestigious Senior Command and Staff College, psc (U). He has successfully undertaken numerous specialized military and civilian ICT training programs, including cyber defence, information security governance, and ICT systems integration, both in Uganda and internationally. His academic and professional development has provided him with a strong foundation to handle complex ICT and security challenges.

Professional Career

Over his distinguished service, he has held multiple senior appointments, including:

  • Head of Communications and Information Security
  • Director of Communication and Information Technology (DCITS) – Land Forces
  • Commanding Officer of various CITS Regiments

In these roles, he has overseen the modernization of secure communication networks, led initiatives to safeguard sensitive military data, and strengthened the UPDF’s cyber resilience capabilities.

Leadership & Contributions

Beyond operational command, Lt Col Okol has been instrumental in:

  • Shaping national ICT and cyber security policies
  • Guiding infrastructure development projects
  • Mentoring young officers and ICT specialists

Through his leadership, the UPDF has made significant progress in the transformation of C4ISR systems, enhancing operational efficiency and preparedness.

Achievements

His outstanding service has earned him respect and recognition for his role in:

  1. Modernizing UPDF’s communication systems
  2. Advancing cyber defence initiatives
  3. Enhancing regional defence cooperation through ICT innovation

Vision & Values

Lt Col Okol is deeply committed to promoting digital transformation, secure communication, and capacity building within the defence sector. He believes in integrity, mentorship, and innovation as guiding principles in service to the nation.
